CryLens Privacy Policy

CryLens helps parents record, analyze, and review baby cry activity. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Account data such as your name, email address, and sign-in method.
  • Baby profiles you create, including names and dates of birth.
  • Cry analysis records, timestamps, labels, confidence scores, and notes.
  • Audio files you choose to upload or record for cry analysis.
  • Subscription and purchase status needed to manage CryLens Pro access.
  • Basic technical data required for app security, diagnostics, and fraud prevention.

How We Use Information

  • To create and secure your account.
  • To process cry recordings and generate analysis results.
  • To sync your baby profiles and history across app sessions.
  • To restore purchases and manage subscription entitlements.
  • To respond to support requests and improve reliability.

AI and Audio Processing

When you request cry analysis, audio you upload may be processed by our backend and AI providers to generate the result. We use that processing only to provide the requested feature and related quality improvements.

Third-Party Services

CryLens may rely on infrastructure providers for authentication, storage, analytics, subscriptions, and AI processing. These providers process information on our behalf to operate the service.

Data Retention

We retain your account data and cry history until you delete your account or request deletion. Some records may be kept longer where required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or transaction auditing.

Your Choices

  • You can update or delete baby profiles in the app.
  • You can delete your CryLens account from the app settings.
  • You can contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data.

Children's Data

CryLens is intended for parents and guardians. It is not designed for direct use by children.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@starklabs.app.
